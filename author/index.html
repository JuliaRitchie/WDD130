How can you get published effectively?

On this website, the goal is to bring writers and publishers on the same site with the publishers posting jobs that writers can send in samples for. Writers will practice different writing skills for outsourced jobs and use those skills to get jobs through this website. Think of it like LinkedIn or other social media platforms.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eliminate the animosity between authors and publishers and to provide a transparent process to help authors get published and publishers to receive the work that they need. A series of prompts will be provided to help guide authors in what samples to write for the publisher to review. Your account will be used to connect with real publishers.
