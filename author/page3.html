Meet the Publishers

Meet Jared Hill, long time publisher of TotallyReal company. Jared works primarily with nonfiction writers to get them technical writing and short-form writing jobs. He has been a part of Finally Published for 4 years and enjoys helping upcoming authors succeed.

This is Stacey Greene, a long time friend of Finally Published who is now a part of the publishing staff. Stacey primarily reaches out to new members of the platform that are looking to get published. Based on surveys, she will match each author to the right publisher. She helps publish nonfiction works.

You aren't ready for Jonah Hill, fiction head publisher. He will transport your story out of this world! Jonah works with those planning to write fiction and helps publish for GenericFantasy. He is always looking for the imaginative, the spooky, the improbable to be the next big hit. If your story is fantastical, then Jonah is your man.

Step back in time with Lilah Day as she helps navigate the world of historical fiction. Lilah works for GemstoneWorks and helps historical fiction and romance authors find their niche. Lilah knows several publishers who are looking for the next romantic story and historical drama. Working with her will help your novel soar above the standards and reach the heights.
